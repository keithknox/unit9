The Knox JiuJitsu Academy is a state of the art facility conveniently located in Alexandria, VA. Classes are centered around Gracie JiuJitsu, submission grappling, and self defense. Students are taught through group classes and personalized attention. The JiuJitsu Academy offers a clean and safe learning environment that everyone from toddler to senior can benefit from. Classes are offered to all ages, regardless of previous knowledge or ability. From the serious competitor to the beginner looking to keep in shape, there is a class for everyone.Be sure to check out our schedule and class information page to find a class that fits your needs.
